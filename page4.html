About Us

All of our investment advice is provided by Dr. Jason Fink. Dr. Fink is a registered adviser representative for Madison Financial Research LLC.**
Jason Fink is a Professor in the Department of Finance and Business Law at James Madison University, where he is also a faculty research fellow. Because of his responsibilities at the university, Madison Financial Research LLC maintains a small number of clients to whom we may provide exemplary service.
After almost two decades in the fields of economics and finance, Dr. Fink has published in numerous academic journals of world-class quality, including the Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis, Financial Management, the Journal of Investment Management, the Journal of Financial Planning, the Journal of Futures Markets, the Journal of Financial Research, and others. At James
Madison University, he teaches courses in derivatives, securities pricing, financial engineering, as well as serving as the primary adviser in the prestigious Quantitative Finance program. He also teaches a quantitative method course in
the Information Security MBA program, recently ranked the #1 such online MBA program in the country.
